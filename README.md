# 🍽️ Nourish

**Free. Open Source. Forever.**

Nourish is an AI-powered personal chef, nutritionist, and fitness coach — all in one app. It generates delicious, healthy, chef-quality meals tailored to your goals, tracks your nutrition, and helps you build a sustainable fitness routine.

No subscriptions. No ads. No corporate BS. Just real food and real health, for everyone.

---

## Why Nourish?

Most health apps make you choose between eating well and eating healthy. Nourish doesn't. Using AI trained on culinary science and nutritional data, it builds meal plans that are genuinely delicious AND nourishing — because food should never feel like punishment.

---

## What It Will Do

- 🍳 AI meal planning based on your goals, preferences, and dietary needs
- 📊 Nutrition tracking with real data
- 💪 Fitness planning and workout tracking
- 🛒 Automatic grocery list generation
- 🔄 Everything syncs and works together
